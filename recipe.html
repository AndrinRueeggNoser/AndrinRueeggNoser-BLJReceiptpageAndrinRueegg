Protein Pancake Recipe

Ingredients 1 person each

5 g Butter

50 g Flour

0.5 tbsp Brown sugar

0.25 packet Vanilla sugar

0.5 tsp Baking powder

0.06 tsp Salt

0.75 dl Buttermilk or Milk

0.25 Fresh egg

Butter for frying

0.5 tbsp Maple syrup or Honey

20 g Protein pulver


Steps


Step 1: Make a mise en place.

That means you should organise all ingredients you can start out.


Step 2: Mix the dry ingredients.

Mix the flour, sugar, protein powder, vanilla sugar, baking powder and salt in a bowl.


Step 3: Mix the wet ingredients.

Mix the buttermilk and egg in a bowl, add it to the dry substance and mix it until its glazing.


Step 4: Fry the pancakes.

Heat up a pan with butter and fry the pancakes on both sides until they are golden brown.


Step 5: Serve the pancakes.

Serve the pancakes with maple syrup or honey.


Step 6: Enjoy your meal!
